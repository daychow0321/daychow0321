    CCT380 Project

    In a team of four, my teammates and I designed a product that deals with the contacts of elevator buttons during the COVID-19 pandemic.

    A smartphone application that generates QR code to be scanned by a QR code scanner inside the elevator to register floors.

    Low-fidelity Prototype

    The low-fidelity design involves mostly hand-drawn patterns to sketch out the approximate app interface.

    Mid-fidelity Prototype

    The Mid-fidelity design was designed in Figma, which involves more shapes. The app interface can also be tested in the way of hyperlinks provided by Figma.

    Keyboards

    KBD67

    This is the first ever custom keyboard I built by myself.

    The case is aluminum. The switches, keycaps, stablizers and the case etc. are all purchased and separately, they are all built together to become one piece.

    I used cream switches for this keyboard. It sounds high in tone. It could be very loud to type on at night.

    KBD67 MK2

    This is the 2nd genaration of my previous custom keyboard.

    The case has a diffrent shape and it is polycarbonate. I also used a different kind of switch, gateron ink switches, which feels smoother than cream swiches but it sounds a little deeper in tone.

    HHKB Pro2

    This is a japanese brand keyboard with topre swtiches.

    It sounds the most quiet and has a very soft touch.
